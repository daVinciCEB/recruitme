About RecruitMe

Swipe your way to your next job!

JobSeekers

JobSeekers (people looking for jobs) create profiles based on their skills, education, and previous experience.

Recruiters

Recruiters (people looking for candidates for jobs) create Jobs with preferences for skills, education, and previous experience.

Match Rankings

The Match Ranking is calculated for each JobSeeker/Job Combination. The higher the Match Ranking, the better the Job for the JobSeeker.

Pricing

JobSeekers and Recruiters all have a Basic Plan for free, as well as the option to upgrade to premium plans.


  • Basic Plan
  • Free
  • The free basic plan for Recruiters and JobSeekers!
  • Recruiters get up to 3 job postings
  • JobSeekers can search up to a 100 mile radius for jobs
  • When a match occurs, make contact to set up calls and next steps
  • Get Started
  • RecruitMe Plus
  • $10/month
  • Add some extra features to your RecruitMe account!
  • Recruiters get up to 10 job postings
  • JobSeekers can search within the entire country that they are in for jobs
  • Contact up to 10 Jobs/JobSeekers immediately, without waiting for a match to occur
  • Notify Jobs/JobSeekers immediately that you are interested in them
  • Get Started

  • RecruitMe Premium
  • $18/m
  • Unlock the full power of RecruitMe to find or fill your next job!
  • Recruiters get an unlimited number of job postings
  • JobSeekers can search anywhere in the world for jobs
  • Contact any Job/JobSeeker immediately, without waiting for a match to occur
  • Get Started
